# Gulf Energy Intelligence Platform (GEIP)

Physical commodity market analytics covering the 
South Asia · Gulf · Africa energy corridor.

## The Corridor Thesis

India's structural pivot toward Russian discounted crude has permanently
displaced Gulf barrels from their dominant Asian market, accelerating a
Gulf-Africa commercial realignment while simultaneously exposing South
Asia's dollar-constrained LNG dependency. These three dynamics form an
integrated corridor that almost no existing research institution covers
in unified form. That is the research gap this platform addresses.

## What This Platform Tracks

- EIA Weekly Petroleum Status Report — automated data pipeline
- Brent crude scenario framework — updated weekly with probability weights
- US crude inventory vs 5-year seasonal average — Wednesday publication chart
- Corridor geopolitical risk monitor — Hormuz, Bab-el-Mandeb, Red Sea
- JKM LNG vs Henry Hub spread — South Asian import cost indicator
